About the Language

In the modern world, both on the internet and in daily life, there is an overwhelming amount of meaningless text that fills space and occupies readers' time without providing any useful information. These texts, despite taking up large volumes, often hold no value and only serve to distract. The concept of Nulltextlang is to rid the world of such contentless texts by packaging them into compact, easily digestible phrases. Nulltextlang helps convey the essence of such texts, leaving only the most general yet necessary information, without excess "nonsense" or "fluff".

Nulltextlang was created spontaneously as a fun tool for expressing the essence of such texts. Its main idea is to pack contentless "fluff" into compact, easily perceived phrases. This makes Nulltextlang the perfect tool for cases where you want to write a lot of text without specific meaning, express a confusing or abstract thought, or simply make a joke.

Nulltextlang is not only a way to simplify meaningless texts but also an opportunity to look at communication from a different angle, giving even the most ordinary words a touch of irony and humor.
